1The NonActivePlayers simultaneously:
1Write a single clue word on their Easel
1⚠ No communication between Players is allowed.

1⚠ Digits, numbers, acronyms, onomatopoeia and special characters are all considered words
1⚠ Invalid words include:
1The MysteryWord, but written differently or written in a foreign language
1A word from the same general family as the MysteryWord ( "prince" and "princess" )
1An invented word
1A word phonetically identical to the MysteryWord ( "whether" and "weather" )
